Minecraft: A Creativity Goldmine


In this site I will discuss the gameplay of Minecraft as well as the soundscape which the game occupies.

Introduction


Minecraft is an open sandbox game that allows each player to push the game past worldly limits.

Players explore a world similar to ours, but they aren't confined to our world's rules.

Instead, they are offered a wide variety of materials and blocks to build with.

The game also features different checkpoints such as defeating the wither or Enderdragon that help the game

still have direction in completing it, even so, I believe the creative aspect of the game to be its main selling point.

Gameplay


To progress in Minecraft players must fight different mobs, build, and destroy in order to defeat the Enderdragon and beat the game.

While the game does have a direction, how the game is played is up to the players themselves.

Some players may find joy in the process, building whatever their heart desires, while others play the game for sport to see how

fast they can complete the game.
